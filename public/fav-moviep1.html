The Best Movie According To Riya Patel

My top 3 all time favourite movies

Animal

"Animal" is a Bollywood thriller about Shankar, a powerful gangster, facing off against a rival gang led by Kunal Khemu. The story takes a dramatic turn when Shankar's long-lost son (Anil Kapoor) returns for revenge. It's a tale of family, loyalty, and the price of power, culminating in a gripping showdown that changes lives forever.

Salar

"Salar" stars Prabhas as a fierce mafia boss who falls in love with Meenakshi (Shruti Haasan). Their romance is challenged by Salar's enemies, led by a rival gang headed by Suniel Shetty. The film is packed with action, drama, and a quest for redemption as Salar navigates love and the treacherous underworld to protect his loved ones.

kuch kuch hota hai!!!

"Kuch Kuch Hota Hai" is a Bollywood classic about Rahul (Shah Rukh Khan) and Anjali (Kajol), college best friends. Their lives change when Tina (Rani Mukerji) enters and Rahul falls for her. Tragedy strikes when Tina passes, leaving a daughter, also named Anjali. Years later, young Anjali discovers her mother's letters and sets out to reunite her father with his first love. It's a heartwarming story of love, friendship, and second chances.
